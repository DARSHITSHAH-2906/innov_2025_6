ABOUT US


The AYUSH sector relies heavily on medicinal plants and herbs, which form the backbone of traditional healing practices. Our Virtual Herbal Garden allows you to explore these plants from the comfort of your home. Discover their medicinal properties, cultivation techniques, and historical significance in traditional medicine.

Strengthen your body's natural defenses and prevent illness.
